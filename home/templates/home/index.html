Welcome to Sanjeevani Hospital Pharmacy

Our features

  1. We provide HOME DELIVERY SERVICE with up to 12% discount (*conditions apply) for our patients. No extra charges or taxes. We are attentive when prescribing drugs.
  2. A registered pharmacist scrutinizes the prescription and dispenses the drugs after explaining doses to the patient or a family member.
  3. A wide selection of medicines is available at our pharmacy.
  4. If our patients’ needs are beyond our selections, we will place the order on their behalf to the nearby stockists.
  5. We offer 17 kinds of Vaccinations at our Pharmacy. Review our Vaccination List.
  6. Our Pharmacy is temperature maintained, dust-free, and humidity-controlled per standards of Department of Drug Administration Nepal Government and FIP.
  7. Patients can wait in our clean and comfortable sitting area after placing the order.
  8. We are open Sunday to Friday from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. and from 7 a.m. to 11 a.m. on Saturdays.

Our Services

Ambulatory Care

We deliver personal health care services on an outpatient(patients not admitted in hospital) basis including diagnosis, observation, treatment, and rehabilitation services.

Laboratory

Tests are carried out on clinical specimens to obtain information about the health of a patient to aid in diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease fast and easily.

Ambulance Service

Our aims to operate fully equipped and staffed ambulances via a central dispatch facility with radio communication between area hospitals and ambulances in order to ensure rapid transport and treatment for individual patients.

Radiology

The Department of Radiology provides state-of-the-art radiologic imaging and therapeutic procedures for patients, offering out students to conduct cutting-edge research to push the barriers of imaging and advance medicine.

Emergency Care

We have a 24 hours Emergency managed by Doctors and staffs to provide advice and assistance to those in need.

Pharmacy

The Pharmacy department offers a wide range of Nepal Government approved medicines to our patients including home medicine delivery services around Biratnagar. After proving your prescription, you can place your order online, by email, phone call, Viber.
